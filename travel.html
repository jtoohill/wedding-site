Travel Info

As a reminder to those traveling from outside Canada, be sure to have a current passport.

Getting there

The closest major airport for Vancouver is YVR (Vancouver International Airport). The airport connects with the Skytrain transit system that will take you to downtown in about 30 minutes.

Accomodation

We recommend staying in the downtown area of Vancouver, as there is lots to see and do and good public transit. Any hotel that is walking distance to a Skytrain station is a good choice.

Getting around

We will provide a shuttle from downtown Vancouver to the venue. Shuttles will leave from downtown Vancouver (location TBD) at 2:00pm and 2:30pm. Return shuttles will depart from the reception venue at 10:00pm and 11:00pm.

If you are staying downtown, it's fairly easy to use public transit (Skytrain and bus) to get around. If you are staying outside of the downtown core, you will most likely need a car as there is no public transit to the venue. Lyft and Uber don't operate in Vancouver, so don't plan on using them!
